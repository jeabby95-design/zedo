About Zędo World

Zedo World is a family of characters designed to make Edo language learning joyful, consistent, and interactive for children. Through stories, videos, songs, and playful activities, each character supports your child’s learning journey in a familiar way. Consistency is vital when teaching children, and Zedo World helps make learning feel safe, fun, and repeatable.

Meet the Zędo World Family

Curious and playful, Eniye helps children learn everyday words through games and repetition.

Zedo was created to help children learn Edo in a simple, joyful way where maintaining language and culture can feel difficult. We understand that many parents want their children to speak Edo but may not always have the time, confidence, or resources to teach it. Busy schedules, limited resources, and language gaps can make it challenging. That’s why we built Zedo. Zedo bridges that gap through interactive tools, audio support, and engaging learning materials. Zedo was created for families who want their children to grow up connected to their language and culture — even when living far from home. Our mission is simple: to make learning Edo language joyful, accessible, and natural for children everywhere. Through posters, interactive flashcards, audio pronunciation, books, videos, and engaging learning materials, Zedo helps children build vocabulary, confidence, and cultural connection — one word at a time.
